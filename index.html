❓ FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Everything about JustSwap — swaps, liquidity, staking, lending, and security.

What is JustSwap? +
JustSwap is a decentralized exchange (DEX) on the TRON blockchain. It allows users to swap TRC-20 tokens, provide liquidity, stake LP tokens, and borrow crypto in a non-custodial manner.
How do I swap tokens on JustSwap? +
Connect your TronLink wallet, select the token you want to swap from and to, enter the amount, and confirm the transaction. The swap will execute with low slippage and a 0.05% fee.
What tokens are supported on JustSwap? +
JustSwap supports all TRC-20 tokens. Popular ones include TRX, USDT, USDC, BTT, SUN, JST, WIN, and many more. You can swap any pair as long as there is liquidity.
How do I add liquidity to a pool? +
Go to the "Pool" tab, select the pair you want, enter the amounts of both tokens, and confirm. You will receive LP tokens representing your share of the pool.
What are LP tokens? +
LP (Liquidity Provider) tokens are issued when you deposit into a liquidity pool. They represent your proportional ownership of the pool and can be staked to earn additional rewards.
How do I stake LP tokens? +
Go to the "Staking" tab, choose a staking pool that accepts your LP tokens, enter the amount, and stake. You'll earn rewards in JST or other tokens.
What is the reward APR for staking? +
APR varies by pool. Currently, TRX/USDT LP staking offers up to 25% APR, USDT/USDC LP around 20%, and TRX/BTT LP up to 28%. Rates are dynamic and subject to change.
Can I borrow crypto on JustSwap? +
Yes, JustSwap offers lending and borrowing services. You can deposit collateral (e.g., TRX) and borrow stablecoins like USDT or USDC with competitive interest rates.
What is the maximum LTV (Loan-to-Value) for borrowing? +
LTV depends on the collateral asset. For TRX, it's 60%, meaning you can borrow up to 60% of your collateral's value. Stablecoin collateral can have up to 80% LTV.
What happens if my loan is liquidated? +
If your collateral value drops below the liquidation threshold (e.g., 75% LTV), your collateral may be sold to cover the loan. Always monitor your positions to avoid liquidation.
What fees does JustSwap charge? +
Swap fee is 0.05% per trade. Liquidity providers earn 0.04% of that, while 0.01% goes to the treasury. Borrowing interest rates start at 4.5% APR.
Is JustSwap safe? +
JustSwap smart contracts have been audited by CertiK, PeckShield, and Hacken. The platform is non-custodial, and funds are always under your control. Over $1.2B TVL with zero security incidents.
Which wallet can I use with JustSwap? +
TronLink is the recommended wallet. You can also use any TRON-compatible wallet that supports dApp integration, such as Klever or TokenPocket.
Do I need TRX for gas fees? +
Yes, TRX is required to pay for transaction fees (energy and bandwidth) on the TRON network. Make sure you have a small amount of TRX in your wallet.
How fast are transactions on JustSwap? +
TRON's block time is ~3 seconds, so most swaps and transactions confirm within seconds. Network congestion may cause slight delays.
Can I trade volatile tokens on JustSwap? +
Yes, you can swap any TRC-20 token, including volatile ones. However, stablecoin pairs offer lower slippage and are more suitable for large trades.
What is the difference between JustSwap and SunSwap? +
Both are TRON DEXs, but JustSwap offers additional features like staking, lending, and borrowing in one integrated platform. SunSwap focuses mainly on swapping and pools.
How do I earn yield on JustSwap? +
You can earn yield by providing liquidity (earning swap fees) and staking your LP tokens to earn additional rewards in JST or other tokens. The combination can yield up to 25%+ APR.
What is the JST token used for? +
JST is the governance token of JustSwap. It is used for staking rewards, voting on proposals, and can be traded on the platform.
How do I claim staking rewards? +
Go to the Staking tab, click "Claim" on your active staking position. Rewards are automatically added to your wallet.
Can I unstake my LP tokens anytime? +
Yes, you can unstake at any time. However, some pools may have a cooldown period (e.g., 7 days) to prevent flash staking. Check the pool details.
Is there a minimum amount for liquidity provision? +
No minimum, but small amounts may not earn significant fees. It's recommended to provide at least a few hundred dollars worth of tokens.
What happens to my LP tokens if I remove liquidity? +
When you remove liquidity, your LP tokens are burned, and you receive back the proportional share of the pool assets (including earned fees).
Can I swap using a mobile wallet? +
Yes, TronLink mobile app supports dApp browsing. You can access JustSwap directly from the mobile browser or within TronLink's dApp section.
How is the swap price determined? +
Prices are determined by the automated market maker (AMM) algorithm based on the liquidity pool's reserves. The price adjusts with each trade to maintain balance.
What is impermanent loss? +
Impermanent loss occurs when the price ratio of the two tokens in a pool changes compared to when you deposited. It can reduce your returns, but trading fees and rewards often compensate.
Does JustSwap have a governance mechanism? +
Yes, JST holders can vote on proposals for fee changes, new pools, and other platform parameters. Governance is fully on-chain.
Can I trade with leverage on JustSwap? +
Currently, JustSwap does not offer leverage trading. It focuses on spot swaps, liquidity provision, staking, and lending.
How do I check my transaction history? +
You can view your transaction history on TRONSCAN by entering your wallet address. JustSwap does not store user data; all transactions are on-chain.
What is the maximum slippage tolerance? +
By default, slippage tolerance is set to 0.5%. You can adjust it in the settings if you need higher tolerance for volatile markets.
How do I add a custom token to the swap list? +
You can manually enter the token contract address in the swap interface. If the token is not listed, you may need to add it to your wallet first.
What is the minimum swap amount? +
There is no strict minimum, but very small amounts may not be economical due to network fees. Typically, swaps of $10 or more are recommended.
Can I use JustSwap without connecting a wallet? +
You can browse the platform and view data without connecting, but to perform any transaction (swap, pool, stake, borrow), you must connect your wallet.
How do I contact support for JustSwap? +
Join our official Telegram or Discord communities for support. Be cautious of scams; we never ask for your private keys.
