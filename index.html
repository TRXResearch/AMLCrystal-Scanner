⚡ WHY TRON

Why swap on TRON instead of Ethereum

💸

Sub‑cent transaction costs

TRON's Bandwidth/Energy model keeps routine swaps far cheaper than typical Ethereum gas fees.

⚡

~3 second block times

TRON produces blocks every ~3 seconds, so swaps and liquidity actions confirm quickly.

🔑

Non‑custodial by design

JustSwap never takes custody of your funds — you sign every transaction from your own wallet.
